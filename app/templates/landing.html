What is Helix ?

Helix is the Inter College Techno-Management fest of Jabalpur Engineering College. It is a platform where experience empowers intellect. Helix was started in 2012-13 as a conference where technology and design converged. Helix today shares the ideas from Technology (science) to business to social issues.

The event is about spreading ideas in full capacity in various diverse fields like technology, creativity, motivation, social, entrepreneurship and many more. The basic motive behind this fest is to provide an opportunity for the overall development of the students.

Helix provides stage to the well known speakers of different domains for the benefit of students of Jabalpur Engineering College. Speakers of this fest are proclaimed expertise of their field. Sharing their experience will help immensely the students in every walk of life.

Helix aims at exploring minds of students and sending a thought provoking wave for the betterment of the bigger picture.
